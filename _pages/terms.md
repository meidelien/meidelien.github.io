## Privacy Policy

The privacy of my visitors is important. This site does not use visitor analytics or custom tracking.

First and foremost, I will never share your email address or any other personal information to anyone without your direct consent.

### Website Tracking

This site does not load analytics scripts, advertising trackers, or custom visitor-logging code.

The hosting platform and any third-party services you choose to use from your own browser may still process requests under their own policies, but there is no site-specific visitor tracking configured here.
